# Dance Clash

> All styles. One floor. Dance Clash.

A high-energy, youth-led battle platform where versatility, creativity, endurance and crowd connection define who takes the win.

## About

Dance Clash is an all-styles dance battle platform designed to celebrate versatility, creativity, and long-term cultural relevance. Dancers from multiple disciplines go head-to-head in structured rounds built for both unforgettable live moments and high-performing digital content.

Powered by [Sturdy Off](https://sturdyoff.com) — a global youth culture and dance platform with over 1 billion views and a community of 3+ million followers.
